Project Description

The core of our problem centered around the many students at Loyola, either on or off campus, that do not have a car. It is often hard to find rides to get to various places such as going to class, grocery shopping, etc. Also, taking multiple trips to such places of common interest and value is not sustainable.

Our project aims to address this problem. We created a website for a ride share service in which we are able to connect students with and without cars to communicate to share rides. Drivers earn extra money for places they were already planning on going to and student riders are able to get to the places they need while spending less money than they would on a traditional ride share service like Uber or Lyft.
